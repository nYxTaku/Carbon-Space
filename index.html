What is Carbon Footprint?

A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases (including carbon dioxide and methane)
that are generated by our actions. Countries can reduce their carbon footprint by -
Shifting from fossil fuel to renewable sources, Expanding public transportation and electric vehicles and,
Retrofit buildings to become energy efficent.


Let's Play a Game

This is a game that can help raise awareness about the climate change.

Use the left and right arrow keys to move your cloud.
Use the cloud to deflect carbon dioxide back into Earth and away from our atmosphere.
You will be slowly earning money. When you have enough, click on something to buy it.
This will slowly reduce your carbon foot print and put less carbon dioxide into the air.
The trees will die twenty seconds after you buy them. Everything else stays forever.
Get your carbon footprint down to 0 to win! Good Luck!!!
